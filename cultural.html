Chinese New Year


Chinese new year, also called Lunar new year begins with the new moon that occurs sometime between January 21 and February 20. During New year, the main thing people do are putting up lots of red spring couplets and lanterns for decoration, make delicious food like dumplings, and giving red envelopes to young ones. There will be fireworks and spending time with family and friends.
